The Commvault Opportunity: A unique approach to Ransomware protection, detection and recovery

With Commvault’s Threat Protection and Recovery Solutions, you can help customers reduce their risk for cyber-attacks and strengthen data security to ensure your business is secure. Gartner highlights Commvault’s unique innovation and differentiation to provide customers industry-leading ransomware protection and recovery solutions.

Your customers will be able to:

Protect

Protect the most important assets including applications, databases, virtual machines, and large file systems, anywhere.

Secure

Secure and expand backup infrastructure capabilities to ensure data is accessible and actionable.

Monitor

Monitor all types of risks and threats and enable recovery quickly if networks and systems are hit.

Reduce

Reduce the available attack surface to minimize risk and combine threat prevention with hardware-independent immutability and privileged access management.

Detect

Detect unusual behavior in advance to avoid costly downtime with a threat monitoring framework that monitors the environment and takes preventive action.

Minimize

Minimize business disruption with “threat erase” technology that cleans malware from backup copies, halts backup copy data aging, and isolates security-impacted.
